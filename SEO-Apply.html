Applying SEO

On 11 Feb 2017

Duplicated contents will be flagged as spam by search engines. You will risk getting your website blacklisted and reducing its exposure, thus reducing traffic and ranking.

Each webpage should have a unique title and description. If your site is a resell business, try to come up with original texts instead of copy-pasting from your seller.

Keyword density: (total number of targeted keywords)/(total number of words). If the percentage is too high, your keyword(s) may be considered as spam.

Cloaking: sneakily hide keyword in the background so users wonâ€™t see, but search spiders will find. This may sound like a nice way to fool search engines, but once your site is flagged with cloaking, it will be remove from search results.

Include description and sharing option along with videos to expand linking network. Youtube is a good hosting service for videos. Choosing a good host is important since it affects your ranking indirectly. Slow sites can frustrate users, so they won't stay for a long time, leading to low traffic.

It is recommended to utilize Google Analytics (it's free!) to learn which keywords resulted an increase in sale. It also offers the option to set a goal for your site and tracking your progress to reach that goal.
