Interested in creating eye-catching and user-friendly websites?This career may be the one for you!

Web designer creates and develops websites or web apps. They design the layout, the look, and features of a website. This job is a mix between graphic design and programming. Their duties include, but are not limited to:

  • Creating content for their websites.
  • Designing web layout.
  • Performing maintenance and update.
  • Troubleshooting when needed.

Web designing often requires you to be both creative and technical. It may sound hard to mix the two at first, but you can slowly learn and adapt, starting from exploring this website. Once you have mastered the basics that our site offers, you will have been able to start an entry-level job!

What if you are not a good self-taught person, or what you found on this site are not enough for you? Do not worry. You still have the choice to enroll in the CIT-82: Web Technologies course, which will prepare you with even more in-depth knowledge concerning web developing.

No matter what option you may take, hopefully you will achieve the goal you had in mind when accessing this website.
