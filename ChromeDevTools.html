Chrome Developer Tools

On 23 Jan 2017

I've found a series of video about Chrome DevTools by Nodecasts, and I think this video below can help me, as well as any beginner, to understand what Chrome DevTools is and what it can do. The tool is like a mini editor that allows you to inspect all elements on the webpage in a clean manner. You can also edit the page's styling and view the changes live on your browser. Another plus point is that DevTools kind of let you preview your page for responsive design, where your page appears smaller than it is on computer web browser.

 

I tried to change how the link on the page would look when it's hovered.

 

On the left side, that is how the page would look like on an iPhone 6 screen (320 x 568).
