Becoming UI/UX Designer

On 9 Feb 2017

The video provided more insight to another branch of web designing. I've heard of UI/UX design before, but I didn't know that UI/UX designer can stand as an independant job position. As I look further into UI/UX design, I notice that even though UI and UX are grouped together, they're still two different aspect of web designing. To keep it simple, UI is designing the aesthetics of a website, while UX is concerned with its usability (is it easy or difficult to use/navigate?). Learn more.

Another term that I wanted to look up was "wireframe". Wireframe is sort of an architectual blueprint for a website. It depicts the page layout, or arrangement, of the site. It focuses on what the screen does, how it will react to users' action, rather than how the site simply looks like. Learn more.
